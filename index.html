// Our Programs

Programs for Every Young Hero

From first blocks of code to real robots — every stage has a program designed for your child's age and curiosity.

🧩
Ages 5–7

Little Coders

First steps in logic and creativity through playful block coding and simple robot kits — no reading required to start building.

🎮
Ages 7–10

Junior Builders

Scratch, game design, and beginner robotics with sensors and motors. Kids design their own characters, levels, and machines.

🤖
Ages 10–14

Robotics Masters

Python and micro:bit/Arduino robotics for kids ready to build real hardware — from line-following robots to smart sensors.

🧠

AI & Minecraft Coding

Kid-friendly introductions to artificial intelligence and Minecraft modding that turn coding concepts into playable adventures.

🏆

Hero Hackathons

Weekend team challenges where young coders pitch an idea, build it together, and present it to family and friends.

☀️

Summer & Holiday Camps

Full-day camps packed with coding, robotics, and hands-on making — the most fun your child will have during the break.

🎤

Leadership & Presentation Skills

Bilingual workshops in Italian, English, and Arabic that build confidence in public speaking and presenting projects.
